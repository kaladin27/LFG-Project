LFG! - README Welcome to LFG!, a website that aims to connect gamers and players across multiple platforms. Our platform provides various features to bring gamers together and enhance their gaming experience.


About Us Our mission is to create a community that enables gamers to connect and interact with each other seamlessly. We strive to break down the barriers between players and platforms and build a welcoming environment for all. We believe that gaming should be inclusive and that everyone should have the opportunity to find their perfect gaming partner.


Connect with the Community Our community is active on Discord. We invite you to join us and meet like-minded gamers, participate in events, and chat about your favorite games. You can join our Discord server by clicking the link on the "Connect with the Community" page.


IRL Meetups We understand that gamers also like to meet up in person and socialize. Our website provides a platform where groups of gamers can plan and organize meetups in real life. You can find the IRL Meetups page on our website.


Share We encourage you to share our website with your friends on Facebook, Twitter, and Discord. You can find the Share page on our website, which contains links to all three social media platforms.


Rules to Abide by! To ensure that our community remains inclusive, friendly, and fun for everyone, we have a set of rules that all users must abide by. You can find the Rules to Abide by! page on our website. Please read these rules carefully before using our platform.


Thank you for choosing LFG! as your go-to platform for finding gaming partners. We hope you enjoy your time here, and we look forward to connecting with you!
