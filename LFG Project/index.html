Whether your looking for a Fireteam to enter the Crucible in Destiny, a group of adventurers to foil your BBEG's nefarious plans, or a group to go LARPing with; we make it easy to find other people that share your interests!

IRL Rules!

In order to ensure the safety and enjoyment of everyone there are TWO VERY IMPORTANT RULES that you must agree to:

Be Respectful and Kind. Simple and sweet. Respect eachothers boundries/space and don't be a troll.
Be Courteous to those around you and especially to any staff working at the venue you are attending. You represent not only your group but the LFG brand as a whole.
